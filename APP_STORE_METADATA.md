## Description (4000 characters max)

```
Capture moments. Watch them develop. Share the magic.

polaGram transforms your photos into authentic instant-style memories, complete with the nostalgic experience of watching your image slowly appear.

THE EXPERIENCE
Tap to capture a photo or choose from your library. Then watch as your image develops over 20 seconds, just like the real thing. Can't wait? Shake your phone to speed up the process – feel the satisfying haptic feedback as your photo comes to life.

VINTAGE CHARACTER
Every photo tells a story, and sometimes that story includes a coffee ring stain, a crease from being kept in a wallet, or the faded edges of a sun-bleached memory. polaGram randomly applies authentic vintage effects to give each photo unique character:

• Coffee stains and water damage
• Creases, folds, and dog-eared corners
• Burn marks and age spots
• Scratches and fingerprints
• Light leaks and chemical spills

Most photos get subtle aging. Some get more. And very rarely (1% chance), you'll get the "shoebox special" – a photo that looks like it survived being crumpled up, spilled on, and forgotten for decades. The image is still there, just with a lot of character.

ADD YOUR MARK
Tap below your photo to add a handwritten caption (24 characters). The worn typewriter effect makes every caption feel personal and authentic.

SHARE THE MOMENT
• Save to Photos with one tap
• Send via Messages
• Share directly to Instagram Stories
• Build your personal gallery of memories

PRIVACY FIRST
Your photos never leave your device. No accounts, no cloud uploads, no tracking. Just you and your memories.

FEATURES
✓ Native camera integration
✓ 20-second development animation
✓ Shake to develop faster
✓ 50 vintage filter effects
✓ Handwritten captions
✓ Haptic feedback
✓ Sound effects
✓ Local gallery with SwiftData
✓ Instagram Stories sharing
✓ Optional watermark
✓ VoiceOver accessible
✓ Works offline

Perfect for:
• Capturing everyday moments with style
• Creating nostalgic photo gifts
• Adding character to your social posts
• Anyone who misses the magic of instant photography

Download polaGram and rediscover the joy of watching a moment develop.
```
