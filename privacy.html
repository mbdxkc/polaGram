Privacy Policy

polaGram
Effective Date: March 7, 2026
Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Overview

polaGram is a photo app that creates instant-style photos with vintage effects. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it's used.

Data Collection

polaGram does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data stays on your device.

Device Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

Permission Purpose Data Handling
Camera Take photos for polas Photos processed locally, never uploaded
Photo Library (Read) Choose existing photos Selected photos processed locally only
Photo Library (Write) Save finished polas Saved directly to your device
Motion Sensors Shake detection, parallax effects Motion data processed in real-time, never stored

Local Storage

  • Saved Polas: Stored locally on your device using iOS/macOS built-in storage
  • Preferences: App settings (watermark toggle, onboarding status) stored locally
  • No Cloud Sync: polaGram does not sync data to any cloud service

Third-Party Sharing

When you choose to share a pola:

  • Messages: Opens iOS Messages app with your pola attached
  • Instagram Stories: Opens Instagram app with your pola (requires Instagram installed)
  • Photos: Saves to your device's photo library

polaGram does not share data with advertisers, analytics services, or any third parties.

Analytics & Tracking

  • No analytics SDKs
  • No crash reporting services
  • No advertising frameworks
  • No user tracking of any kind

Children's Privacy

polaGram does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Delete saved polas anytime from the Gallery
  • Uninstalling the app removes all polaGram data

Changes to This Policy

Updates will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.
